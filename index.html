About Special K

Special K is a breakfast cereal made by Kellogg's. It is known for its crispy flakes and delicious taste.

Nutrition Facts

One serving of Special K (1 cup, 31g) contains:

  • Calories: 120
  • Total Fat: 0g
  • Cholesterol: 0mg
  • Sodium: 200mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 27g
  • Dietary Fiber: 0g
  • Sugars: 5g
  • Protein: 2g
